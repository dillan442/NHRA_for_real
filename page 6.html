Legends of the sport

There are many people in the sport that are famous for one or more reasons and here is a list of the few that I can remember

John Force

John has 16 NHRA championships in the Funny Car class and is easily the most liked active racer. To make his career even better, he came very poor beginnings. He lived on a farm in the middle of nowhere but he didn't let that stop him from reaching his goals.

Shirley Muldowney

Shirley is the first female to drive a drag car that is capable of goingover 150 MPH, first female to break the 6-second barrier, named driver of the year in 1976, first woman to win an NHRA championship, first woman to win 3 NHRA championships, and many, many more.

Don Prudhomme

He won his first professional event at the age of 20, first driver to win the Winter Nationals and U.S. Nationals in the same season, second driver to ever win the U.S. Nationals two years in a row, first to break the 6-second barrier

Bob Glidden

He has the third most NHRA event wins at 85, he is behind Warren Johnson and John Force, he won 10 NHRA championships in his pro stock class and five of these were won back-to-back, he also won 9 straight NHRA events

Warren Johnson

He has the the most event wins in the pro stock class at 97, first NHRA pro stock driver to exceed 200 MPH, and the first pro stock driver to make a sub-6 second pass
