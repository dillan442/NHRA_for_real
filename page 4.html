These cars only got faster

As of right now, the fastest car in the NHRA and the fastest accelerating vehicle on earth is the Top Fuel Dragster. The name may sound odd and a little funny, but this car means business and isn't something to be messed with. It can reach speeds of up to 330 MPH in under 4 seconds.

These weren't always this fast though. In the early years 330 MPH wasn't even a thought. The length of the track is a quarter mile, or 1,320 FT. At first it would take about 13 seconds maybe even more to reach the finish line. Now the fastest car is capable in doing in in under 4 seconds. I would say that they can't possibly get any faster, but new world records are practically set every year.
